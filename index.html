Architecture

The art or practice of designing and constructing buildings.

Architecture has a relation with space and everything around built structures. Having spent time thinking on how this works in different situations, has helped me understand the relationship with space and built structure. Also reading books and being dedicated to the study of architecture has taught me a few things. Space is important in this growing world, as we lack space in modern urban center's around the world, we find theres less space for our living and working enviroments. Space, and proportion are very important in creating these enviroments. Space helps us feel safe or gives us a place to think and rest, and proportionhelps us put thing in proper place and helps keep things organized. This site was designed to help you understand how this works. Take in the use of space as you scroll down. Keep in mind this site is a visual for some thing that keeps me working at learning arc hitecture for a proffession. Notice the space as you scroll down and compare left to right. This will give you an idea of how build structure fits with space, both positive and negative.
